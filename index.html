Our National Flag

Every country has a national flag of its own. As an independent country, Bangladesh also has a national flag. A national flag is the symbol of freedom of a nation. Our national flag is rectangular. The background of the flag is bottle green and it has a red circle in the middle. The green colour stands for our green fields, everlasting freshness, vigour and vitality of our nation. The red circle stands for red sun of freedom. The blood-red colour of the sun is a memorial of bloody war and sacrifice of 30 lac martyrs lives. The flag’s length and breadth are in the ratio 10:6. We are proud of our country and determined to hold the prestige of our national flag.
